What is Spam Email Attacks Dashboard?

We are team MIMECRAFT of DSCI 550, under the direction of Dr. Chris Mattmann, have worked on three project assignments focusing on analysis of spam emails. As part of our third assignment, we have summarized the features in the form of graphs that stood out to us while analyzing. The tools used for data visualization were D3.js, Elastic Search, Image Space, Geo parser and Apache Tika.

For our analysis we took the output from assignment 1 and 2 and used CSV/TSV/JSON files to build the data script for d3 visualization. We picked 5 types of visualizations: scatterplot, elastic search based piechart, heatmap, zoomable treemap and bar chart. You can refer this dashboard to see our visualizations here - Explore!

Geo Parser Insights


Geoparser view of location of all emails

Geoparser gives user the locations extracted from the contents of the fraudulent emails. Checkout the demo video to know how it looks for our dataset and how effective it is to leverage Geoparser for word search analysis.

Image Space Insights


Image Space detection of similar facial pictures

After we ingested 1600 machine generated facial images, Image Space would pick similar pictures to the one searched from all images. In other words, users can upload a picture, and Image Space returns a group of images with a similar face inside. Checkout the demo video to know how it looks for our dataset and how effective it is to leverage Image Space for multimedia analysis.
